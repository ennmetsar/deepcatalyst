Questions

The fine print, in plain words.

What does it cost?

Nothing, ever, for talent. Companies and funds pay to reach you — and only on terms you've already approved.

Who sees my answers?

No one, until you say yes to a specific opportunity. Your profile is private by default, and a human reviews every match before anything moves.

Will the readout just flatter me?

No. If we don't see founder signal, we say so — and tell you what would change our mind. The readout is only worth screenshotting because it's honest.

What if there's no match?

Your profile waits quietly. Opportunities arrive when they're worth your time, or not at all. You can delete everything whenever you want.

Why deep tech first?

Because it's the hardest matching problem and the biggest prize: real breakthroughs, missing founders. If the AI works here, it works anywhere.

Is this compliant?

Built for GDPR and the EU AI Act from day one: explicit consent, human review of every match, no inference of protected characteristics, full deletion rights.

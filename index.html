Questions

The fine print, in plain words.

What does it cost?

Nothing, ever, for you. Companies pay to reach you — and only on terms you've already agreed to.

Who sees my conversation?

No one, until you say yes to a specific opportunity. Your profile is private by default, and a human reviews any match before it ever reaches you.

Will the readout just flatter me?

No. It's calibrated to what you actually give it — sharp and complimentary if you've earned it, reserved if the conversation was thin. It's only worth keeping because it's honest.

Do I need a CV?

No. It's a conversation — you talk about what you've done, in your own words. If you want an even sharper read you can paste your background in, but nothing is required and nothing is made public.

What if there's no match?

Your profile waits quietly. Opportunities arrive when they're worth your time, or not at all. You can delete everything whenever you want.
